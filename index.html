A bit about me

My name is Daren Schaad. I was born in Portland, OR in 1981. I grew up in NE Portland and have lived there most of my life. In 1999 I started college at Oregon State in corvallis, pursuing my bachelor degree in engineering and physics. I spent 6 months in rural Tanzania teaching math and physics to students at a secondary school. I worked as a mechanical engineer for nearly 10 years here in Portland. Now I am enrolled full time in code school at Epicodus in downtown Portland, OR. I am very excited about this newest chapter in my life!

Traveling Expirience

I am so greatful to my parents who have instilled the love of traveling in me. They both grew up in Angola and then Zaire(now the Democratic Republic of the Congo) and have travelled extensively. In my time on this planet I have been to some pretty amazing places in North America, Central America, South America, Europe, Asia and Africa. When I travel I prefer to rough it for the most part and imerse myself in the culture, fancy resorts aren't my style.

Teaching Experience

For my senior internship at OSU, I decided to teach math and physics in Siha Tanzania, at the base of Mt. Kilimanjaro. I lived there for 6 months teaching students between the ages of 14-22. It was an amazing experience and I still think back fondly on my time there often. Me and another American guy, who was in the same program, were even able to set up a rudimentary computer lab for the students to use. We received donated computers and set the lab up in one of the schools rooms that was not being used.
